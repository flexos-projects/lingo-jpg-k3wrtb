# Vision

## The One-Line
A gamified language learning app that teaches real-world slang and internet culture through meme translation.

## The Problem
Traditional language apps teach textbook vocabulary for tourists, leaving learners functionally illiterate when faced with real-world internet culture, slang, and memes. Google Translate fails to capture the cultural context, puns, and humor inherent in native social media content.

## The Insight
Humor and internet culture are the ultimate tests of fluency. If you can understand a culture's memes, you understand their language. By replacing sterile textbook sentences with viral content, we can weaponize the addictive scroll of social media for education.

## Who It's For
The 11pm scroller who has a 400-day streak on a traditional language app but still can't understand foreign TikTok comments. It is for learners who want to participate in internet culture, not just order a train ticket.
**Explicitly NOT for:** Absolute beginners learning the alphabet, people studying for formal exams (DELE, JLPT), or children.

## What It Is
Lingo.jpg is a dark-mode, TikTok-style feed where every post is a meme in your target language with obscured text. Users must decipher the punchline or slang to earn points. AI provides a "Culture Breakdown" explaining the joke and context. Points are spent in a Vault to unlock localized digital stickers. Saved memes become a contextual flashcard deck.

## What It Is Not
- Not a grammar textbook.
- Not a structured course with "Units" and "Lessons".
- Not an app for learning to ask for directions.
- Not a generic flashcard app (no isolated words without context).

## Why Now
LLMs (like Claude 3.5 Sonnet) are finally capable of parsing cultural nuance, slang, and humor accurately, solving the "Google Translate" gap. Simultaneously, internet culture is more globalized than ever, and learners crave authentic connection over sterile curriculum.

## What Success Looks Like
- **3-Month:** 5,000 active users completing at least 10 meme translations per day. High retention on the "Vault" unlock loop.
- **12-Month:** 100,000 active users. UGC "Field Notes" generating 80% of new curriculum.

## Core Values Embedded in the Product
1. **Cultural context over grammatical perfection:** Slang is valid vocabulary.
2. **Learning should feel like scrolling:** The UI must be frictionless, dark, and immersive.
3. **Explains jokes without ruining them:** The AI voice must be internet-native and irreverent.
4. **Status is the best reward:** Gamification must lead to tangible social flexing (custom emojis).
