# Flows

### The "Aha" Moment
You're scrolling The Feed in bed. A Spanish meme appears. It’s a picture of a dog looking exhausted. The text says: *"Yo después de decir 'no mames' 50 veces en una hora."* The phrase 'no mames' is blanked out. You have to translate "You've got to be kidding me" from English into the blank. 

You type it in. The screen shakes slightly, a satisfying *ding* plays, and the text turns neon green. +50 Points. The Culture Breakdown slides up. It explains that while it literally means something vulgar, in Mexico it's the standard punctuation mark for disbelief. You laugh because you finally *get* it. You tap the bookmark icon to save it to your Deck, and swipe up to the next meme.

### The Vault Flex
You’ve hit a 7-day streak. The streak multiplier just deposited 1,000 Glot Points into your account. You navigate to The Vault. You've been eyeing the "Chilaquiles Reaction" sticker for days. It costs 800 points. You tap it. A loud, arcade-style unlock animation plays. The sticker is yours. 

You immediately go back to The Feed, find a meme about breakfast, open the comments, and drop your newly unlocked Chilaquiles sticker. Two other users reply to your comment with fire emojis. You didn't just learn a word; you bought status in the community.

### The Field Note Upload
You're on Twitter. You see a viral tweet in Japanese that has 100k likes, but you have no idea why it's funny. You screenshot it. You open Lingo.jpg, go to Field Notes, and upload the image. 

A loading state says "Decoding the culture..." After 5 seconds, the AI breaks it down: it identifies the slang, explains that it's a reference to a recent political gaffe, and provides the English equivalent. The app asks: "Turn this into a challenge?" You hit yes. The meme enters the global feed. Whenever someone else solves it, you get a 10% cut of the points they earn. You are now a curriculum contributor.
