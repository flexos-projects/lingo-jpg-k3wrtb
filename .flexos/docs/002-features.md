# Features

## Tier System
**Free Tier:** Unlimited feed scrolling, basic culture breakdowns, standard vault items, ads every 10 memes.
**Pro Tier (Lingo+):** Ad-free, unlimited UGC Field Notes uploads, exclusive premium vault items, advanced slang dictionary saves.

## Feature Areas

### 1. Core Product Loop
- **The Feed:** (Free/Pro) Endless vertical scroll of memes. Users guess obscured text (fill-in-the-blank, scrambled words, or multiple choice).
- **Culture Breakdown:** (Free/Pro) AI-generated explanation of the joke, slang definitions, and literal vs. actual meaning. Opens as a bottom sheet after guessing.

### 2. Gamification & Economy
- **Glot Points System:** (Free/Pro) Points awarded for correct translations, speed, and streak milestones.
- **Slang Streaks:** (Free/Pro) Daily translation streaks that apply multipliers to points earned.
- **The Vault:** (Free/Pro) Storefront to spend Glot Points on custom, culturally specific emojis and sticker packs. Premium items restricted to Pro tier.

### 3. Retention & Study
- **The Deck:** (Free/Pro) Saved memes gallery acting as a contextual spaced-repetition flashcard library.
- **The Comments:** (Free/Pro) Discussion area under each meme to interact with others and flex unlocked Vault emojis.

### 4. User-Generated Content (UGC)
- **Field Notes:** (Free limited / Pro unlimited) Upload wild memes. AI runs OCR, explains the joke, and optionally publishes it to the community feed. Uploader earns a cut of points when others solve it.

## Explicitly Out of Scope
- Voice recognition or pronunciation grading.
- Grammar exercises or verb conjugation tables.
- Direct messaging between users.
- Live multiplayer translation battles.
