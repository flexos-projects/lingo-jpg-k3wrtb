# Features

## Tier System
**Free Tier:** 
- Unlimited feed scrolling.
- Standard culture breakdowns.
- Standard vault items.
- Ads injected every 15 memes.
- Limit: 3 Field Notes (UGC) uploads per day.

**Pro Tier (Lingo+):** 
- $8/month or $60/year.
- 100% Ad-free experience.
- Unlimited UGC Field Notes uploads.
- Exclusive "Premium/Epic" vault items.
- Advanced Deck filtering and unlimited saved items.

## Feature Areas

### 1. Core Product Loop
- **The Feed:** (Free/Pro) Endless vertical scroll of memes. Users guess obscured text. Input types include:
  - *Fill-in-the-blank:* Typing the exact missing word.
  - *Multiple Choice:* 4 options, heavily distractored with literal (but incorrect) translations.
  - *Scrambled Words:* Tapping word blocks in the correct order.
- **Culture Breakdown:** (Free/Pro) AI-generated explanation of the joke, slang definitions, and literal vs. actual meaning. Opens automatically as a bottom sheet after a guess or a "Skip".
- **Haptic Feedback & Audio:** (Free/Pro) Aggressive, satisfying tactile feedback on correct answers, accompanied by arcade-style success chimes.

### 2. Gamification & Economy
- **Glot Points System:** (Free/Pro) The currency. 
  - Base correct answer = +50 pts.
  - Speed bonus (under 5 seconds) = +20 pts.
  - Skip/Incorrect = 0 pts.
- **Slang Streaks:** (Free/Pro) Daily translation streaks. Hitting milestones (7, 14, 30 days) applies a permanent multiplier to points earned (e.g., 1.1x, 1.2x) as long as the streak is alive.
- **The Vault:** (Free/Pro) Storefront to spend Glot Points. Items cost between 500 (Common) and 5,000 (Epic) points. Users buy custom, culturally specific emojis and sticker packs. Premium items restricted to Pro tier.

### 3. Retention & Study
- **The Deck:** (Free/Pro) Saved memes gallery acting as a contextual spaced-repetition (SRS) flashcard library. Uses a modified SuperMemo-2 algorithm. Cards are scheduled for review based on how easily the user recalled the slang.
- **The Comments:** (Free/Pro) Discussion area under each meme to interact with others, ask native speakers for nuance, and flex unlocked Vault emojis.

### 4. User-Generated Content (UGC) & Moderation
- **Field Notes:** Upload screenshots of wild memes. AI runs OCR, translates, explains the joke, and formats it as a challenge.
- **Community Publishing:** Users can toggle "Publish to Feed". If approved, the meme enters the global curriculum. The original uploader earns a 10% royalty on all Glot Points generated by others solving their meme.
- **Community Moderation:** "Flag" button on every feed item for "Incorrect Translation", "Offensive/NSFW", or "Not a Meme". 3 flags automatically pull the meme for human review.

## Explicitly Out of Scope for v1
- Voice recognition, pronunciation grading, or speaking exercises.
- Grammar exercises, verb conjugation tables, or traditional "lessons".
- Direct messaging (DMs) between users.
- Live multiplayer translation battles or leaderboards (focus is on personal progression and economy, not PvP).
- Exporting stickers directly to native iOS/Android keyboards (will rely on standard image share sheets for v1 due to PWA constraints).
