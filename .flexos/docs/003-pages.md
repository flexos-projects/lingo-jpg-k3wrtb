## Page Specifications

### Landing (`/`)
- **Purpose:** Hook users immediately with an interactive meme challenge, explain the value proposition, and drive signups.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Public users arriving from social media ads (TikTok, IG Reels). They are curious, likely frustrated with traditional language apps, and have a short attention span.
- **UX Vision:** High contrast, cyber-neon, zero fluff. Feels like a hacker terminal meets TikTok. Immediate interactivity.
- **Sections (Top to Bottom):**
  1. **Hero Challenge:** Live, interactive meme with a blank. Headline: "Translate this to enter." (Hero-sized, edge-to-edge).
  2. **Social Proof:** "Join 50,000 scrollers." Scrolling marquee of user avatars and unlocked emojis. (Compact band).
  3. **How It Works:** 3-step visual explainer: 1. Scroll the feed. 2. Guess the slang. 3. Unlock the culture. (Grid layout).
  4. **Product Preview:** Autoplaying, high-framerate video of the Culture Breakdown sliding up and haptic success animations.
  5. **Value Props:** Expanded cards. "Stop learning how to order a train ticket. Start learning how to understand the group chat."
  6. **The Vault Teaser:** Showcase of premium stickers pulsing with a neon glow to tease the gamification.
  7. **FAQ:** Objection handling. "Is this for beginners?" (No). "What languages?" (Spanish, French, Japanese).
  8. **Final CTA:** "Enter the Feed." + Sticky footer with signup buttons.
- **Key Interactions:** Solving the hero meme challenge directly on the page, clicking signup.
- **Conversion Goal:** Create an account.

### Auth (`/login`)
- **Purpose:** Frictionless entry and account creation.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users who just solved the hero challenge. They want their dopamine hit and their points. Impatient.
- **UX Vision:** Minimalist, fast, distraction-free.
- **Sections:** 
  - Central modal: Social login buttons (Google, Apple) prominent.
  - Email/password fallback toggle.
  - "Save your 50 points" microcopy.
- **Key Interactions:** OAuth flow.
- **Conversion Goal:** Complete signup/login to save streak.

### The Feed (`/feed`)
- **Purpose:** The core learning loop.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Authenticated users looking for a dopamine hit while learning.
- **UX Vision:** Immersive vertical scroll. Dark mode only. Content is king. No visible navigation chrome while scrolling.
- **Sections:**
  1. **Top Bar:** Streak counter (flames), Points balance. Fades out on scroll down, reappears on slight scroll up.
  2. **Meme Card:** Full-screen image.
  3. **Interaction Zone:** Dynamic based on meme type (Text input, 4-button grid for multiple choice, or draggable word pills).
  4. **Action Bar:** Hints (cost points), Skip, Save to Deck, Comments (with counter).
  5. **Culture Breakdown Drawer:** Hidden by default. Slides up on correct answer or skip. Contains Literal Translation, Actual Meaning, and Tone tags.
- **Key Interactions:** Guessing the translation, swiping vertically to next meme, opening comments, expanding the breakdown.
- **Empty/Loading States:** Skeleton loader matching meme shape with a scanning laser animation. "You've reached the end of the internet" if cache runs out.
- **Conversion Goal:** Keep scrolling and answering.

### The Vault (`/vault`)
- **Purpose:** Spend points on status symbols to close the gamification loop.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users who just hit a streak or accumulated points. Excited, ready to flex.
- **UX Vision:** Neon-lit digital storefront. Arcade vibes. Heavy use of electric purple and glow effects.
- **Sections:**
  1. **Header:** Current Glot Points prominently displayed with a coin icon.
  2. **Featured Drops:** Rare/expensive stickers rotating daily (Hero sized, animated).
  3. **Grid:** Available stickers/emojis categorized by rarity. Silhouettes for locked items.
- **Key Interactions:** Tapping an item to view details, purchasing, watching the explosive unlock animation.
- **Empty States:** N/A (always items to buy).
- **Conversion Goal:** Drain points balance to motivate more learning.

### The Deck (`/deck`)
- **Purpose:** Spaced repetition and review of saved content.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users wanting to solidify their memory or review before a trip.
- **UX Vision:** A slick photo gallery that flips into a serious study tool.
- **Sections:** 
  1. **Stats Header:** "Cards due for review today: 12".
  2. **Grid:** Thumbnails of saved memes.
  3. **Study Mode Overlay:** Full-screen flashcard interface. Shows meme, user must recall slang. Tapping flips card to reveal breakdown and "Ease" buttons (Hard, Good, Easy) to drive the SRS algorithm.
- **Key Interactions:** Flipping cards, grading recall.
- **Empty States:** "Your deck is as empty as a textbook. Go scroll."
- **Conversion Goal:** Complete the daily review queue.

### Field Notes (`/upload`)
- **Purpose:** UGC ingestion and AI analysis.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users who found a meme they don't understand in the wild (Twitter, Reddit).
- **UX Vision:** Camera/upload interface with sci-fi AI scanning effects.
- **Sections:** 
  1. **Upload Zone:** Drag/drop or camera roll picker.
  2. **AI Processing State:** "Decoding the culture..." with scanning line over the image.
  3. **Result Breakdown:** The AI's explanation.
  4. **Publish Toggle:** "Publish to Community for royalties" vs "Keep Private".
- **Key Interactions:** Uploading image, reading AI breakdown, submitting to community.
- **Conversion Goal:** Publish the meme to the global feed.

### Profile (`/profile`)
- **Purpose:** Gamer tag, stats, and settings.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users checking their progress or managing their subscription.
- **UX Vision:** Dashboard-lite. Dense but clear.
