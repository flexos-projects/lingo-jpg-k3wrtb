## Page Specifications

### Landing (`/`)
- **Purpose:** Hook users immediately with an interactive meme challenge and drive signups.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Public users arriving from social media ads. They are curious but skeptical of another language app.
- **UX Vision:** High contrast, cyber-neon, zero fluff. Feels like a hacker terminal meets TikTok. 
- **Sections:**
  1. **Hero Challenge:** Live meme with a blank. "Translate this to enter." (Hero-sized)
  2. **Social Proof:** "Join 50,000 scrollers." (Compact)
  3. **How It Works:** 3-step visual explainer (Translate -> Understand Context -> Flex).
  4. **Product Preview:** Autoplaying video of the Culture Breakdown in action.
  5. **Value Props:** "Stop learning how to order a train ticket. Start learning how to understand the group chat."
  6. **The Vault Teaser:** Showcase of premium stickers.
  7. **FAQ:** Addressing "Is this for beginners?" (No).
  8. **Final CTA:** "Enter the Feed." + Footer.
- **Key Interactions:** Solving the hero meme challenge, clicking signup.
- **Conversion Goal:** Create an account.

### Auth (`/login`)
- **Purpose:** Frictionless entry.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users who just solved the hero challenge and want their points. Impatient.
- **UX Vision:** Minimalist, fast.
- **Sections:** Social login buttons (Google, Apple), Email/password fallback.
- **Key Interactions:** OAuth flow.
- **Conversion Goal:** Complete signup/login to save streak.

### The Feed (`/feed`)
- **Purpose:** The core learning loop.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Authenticated users looking for a dopamine hit while learning.
- **UX Vision:** Immersive vertical scroll. Dark mode only. Content is king.
- **Sections:**
  1. **Top Bar:** Streak counter, Points balance.
  2. **Meme Card:** Full-screen image with interactive text overlay (fill-in-the-blank or scrambled options).
  3. **Action Bar:** Hints, Skip, Save to Deck.
  4. **Culture Breakdown Drawer:** Hidden by default. Slides up on correct answer or skip.
- **Key Interactions:** Guessing the translation, swiping vertically to next meme, opening comments.
- **Empty/Loading States:** Skeleton loader matching meme shape.
- **Conversion Goal:** Keep scrolling and answering.

### The Vault (`/vault`)
- **Purpose:** Spend points on status symbols.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users who just hit a streak or accumulated points. Excited, ready to flex.
- **UX Vision:** Neon-lit digital storefront. Arcade vibes.
- **Sections:**
  1. **Header:** Current Glot Points prominently displayed.
  2. **Featured Drops:** Rare/expensive stickers (Hero sized).
  3. **Grid:** Available stickers/emojis with prices. Silhouettes for locked items.
- **Key Interactions:** Purchasing an item, watching the unlock animation.
- **Empty States:** N/A (always items to buy).
- **Conversion Goal:** Drain points balance to motivate more learning.

### The Deck (`/deck`)
- **Purpose:** Spaced repetition and review.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users wanting to solidify their memory.
- **UX Vision:** Photo gallery that flips into a study tool.
- **Sections:** Grid of saved memes. Tapping flips to vocabulary and slang definitions.
- **Key Interactions:** Flipping cards, marking as "Mastered".
- **Empty States:** "Your deck is as empty as a textbook. Go scroll."
- **Conversion Goal:** Review 10 items.

### Field Notes (`/upload`)
- **Purpose:** UGC ingestion.
- **Audience & Arrival Context:** Users who found a meme they don't understand in the wild.
- **UX Vision:** Camera/upload interface with AI scanning effects.
- **Sections:** Upload zone, AI processing state, Result breakdown, "Publish to Community" toggle.
- **Key Interactions:** Uploading image, reading AI breakdown.
- **Conversion Goal:** Publish the meme to the global feed.

### Profile (`/profile`)
